Yii2 OAuth 2.0

Unlike other implementations, this project is made entirely on the basis of the framework Yii2, with the support of the major design patterns

Feautures

Using behaviors to provide the necessary functionality in your controller. Saving session authorization request gives the option to switch on additional steps, including possible to withdraw authorization from third-party providers, such as Facebook or Google.
